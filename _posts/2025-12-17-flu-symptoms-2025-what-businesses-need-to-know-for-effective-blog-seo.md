## Key Flu Symptoms to Highlight in Your Blog

Accurate, timely content builds trust and drives return visits. For 2025, important flu symptoms to mention include:

- Fever and chills  
- Persistent cough  
- Sore throat  
- Body aches and fatigue  
- Headaches  
- Nasal congestion  
- Occasional gastrointestinal symptoms (especially in children)

Providing actionable advice—like when to seek medical attention or how to prevent the spread of flu—positions your business blog as a reliable source.
