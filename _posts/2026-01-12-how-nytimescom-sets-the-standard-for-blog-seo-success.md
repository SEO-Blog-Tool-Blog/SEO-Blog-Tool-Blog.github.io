- **Structured Content:** Their posts are well-organized, making use of headings, bullet points, and internal links—best practices for any on-page SEO tool.
- **Ongoing Optimization:** Content is regularly updated and optimized, using a systematic SEO checklist for blogs to maintain relevance and ranking power.
